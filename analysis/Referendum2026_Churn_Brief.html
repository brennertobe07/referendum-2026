What it means
  • Drop-off skews young and Democratic. 22% of Dem 2025G voters dropped vs 17% of Republicans, and 42% of 18–24s. The Yes win came despite losing more of the Dem base — cushioned by its size.
  • Surge skews young but slightly Republican. New-to-2025G referendum voters were 11.8% of Rep voters vs 9.8% of Dems — Democrats lost more of their base and replaced less of it.
  • Re-mobilization target: young, Dem-leaning 2025 voters — highest volume in Fairfax / Loudoun / Prince William, highest rate in college towns (Harrisonburg, Charlottesville, Williamsburg). Voter-level list available (held privately, VAN-ready).
Source: 2025-General vote history and 2026 referendum participation matched in the VAN voter file (Van party ID + Dem-support score).
